You will be asked to take a decision in a dilemma. Here are the rules. Read them carefully, they will be part of the quiz.

You and another m-turker are matched in a team. One of you will be in the role of a "manager" and the other will be a "worker".
The chance that you will be a "manager" or a "worker" is 50/50, it will be randomly decided.

The payoff is calculated in tokens. The exchange rate of tokens to US dollar depends on the actions of your team, and the actions of 19 other teams in this experiment. The base exchange rate is 1 token = $1.

- Complying yields 3 tokens for you and 3 tokens for your co-player, the exchange rate stays the same.
- Cheating yields 5 tokens, but the exchange rate for you and 19 other teams in this experiment is decreased by $0.04 per token.

Here are some examples:
• If every team complies, 1 token = $1. All participants receive 3 tokens that are worth $3 in total. This maximizes payoffs for all teams.
• If every team cheats, the exchange rate is decreased to 1 token = $0.20. All participants receive 5 tokens that are worth $1 in total.
• If your team complies, but all 19 other teams cheat, the exchange rate is decreased to 1 token = $0.24. You and your co-player receive 3 tokens that are worth $0.72 in total, all other participants receive 5 tokens that are worth $1.20 in total.
• If your team cheats, but all other teams comply, the exchange rate is 1 token = $0.96. You and your co-player receive 5 tokens that are worth $4.80 in total, all other participants receive 3 tokens that are worth $2.88 in total.

This is the dilemma: Cheating increases the payoff of your team, but it decreases the payoff of everybody.
